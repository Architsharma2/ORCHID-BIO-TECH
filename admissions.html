Admissions & Financial Aid

Join a community where innovation meets opportunity. Start your journey at ORCHID BIO-TECH today.

How to Apply

  1. Review Eligibility: Check the course prerequisites and qualifications.
  2. Fill Online Application: Submit your form through our official admissions portal.
  3. Upload Documents: Academic certificates, photo ID, and personal statements.
  4. Pay Application Fee: Securely through our online system.
  5. Wait for Communication: Admissions team will update you on next steps.

Scholarships & Aid

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for deserving candidates. Financial aid options include tuition waivers, research assistantships, and more.
