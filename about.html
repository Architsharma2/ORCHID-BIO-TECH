About Our Green Campus

At ORCHID BIO-TECH, our campus is not only an educational haven but also a lush ecosystem of diverse plant species.

Mango Tree

Symbol of vitality and growth, mango trees bear fruit seasonally and offer shade to students.

Orchid Blossoms

Delicate and rare, orchids reflect our commitment to precision and the wonders of nature.

Neem Tree

Neem trees represent Ayurvedic roots, offering medicinal and environmental benefits.

Banyan Tree

Symbol of wisdom and strength, banyan trees near our library are ideal for quiet study.

Gulmohar

With bright orange flowers, Gulmohar trees light up our walkways with seasonal charm.
